Faith & Reason Topics

Proof of the Trinity in Early Christianity

Ignatius of Antioch (c. 110 AD) - mentions “Jesus Christ, our God.”

Didache - references baptism “in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.”

See also: Matthew 28:19

Divinity of Jesus in the Bible and History

John 1:1 – “The Word was God.”

Pliny the Younger (c. 112 AD) – Christians worship Christ “as to a god.”

Hebrews 1:8 – “Your throne, O God, is forever and ever.”
